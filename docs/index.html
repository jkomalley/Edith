Edith

About

On Wednesday October 14th, 2020 Pitt had a mental health day where they cancelled all classes and encouraged students to take the day to decompress. I decided to take the day to do something I had wanted to do for a long time, build my own computer. In the weeks leading up to the day I ordered the parts and planned out how I was going to do the build. I also did lots of research online via variaous youtube videos and articles. The final product was a computer I have dubbed Edith.
